ABOUT US

Rehabilitate Children &Youth Ethiopia INC. is a non-profit organization, classified as a private foundation by the Department of the Treasury Internal Revenue Service, and based in Jacksonville Florida, U.S.
The founder, Altaye A. Alambo, is a former Ethiopian senior diplomat and researcher, committed to the wellbeing of Children and Youth in Ethiopia. The Altaye Foundation aims to support the helpless Children and Youth in Ethiopia, which may contribute to national development.
